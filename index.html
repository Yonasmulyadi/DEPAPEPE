DEPAPEPE

Acoustic guitar duo

About

Depapepe is one of the most popular music groups from Japan, which is established since 2002 by two acoustic guitar players, Takuya Miura and Yoshinari Tokuoka. The name "Depapepe" itself comes from the combination of both musicians' nickname; "Deppa" from Takuya, which means "overbite", and "Pepe" from Yoshinari's previous band "DERUPEPE". They made their major debut in 2005 via their first album titled "Let's Go!" and up until now, they have released more than 10 albums, with many listed in the Japan's top charts right after its release.
